About us

WHAT ARE WE?

Welcome to the vibrant hub of creativity and expression - Our Oshwal Academy Nairobi Senior High's Media Hub! As a diverse community of budding programmers, writers, photographers, and gamers, we come together to explore the vast and ever-evolving world of media. Dive into our virtual space where pixels meet passion, words dance on screens, and every frame tells a unique story. We host many events and competitions throughout the year from photography to programming and gaming.

Find Out More

What we do

At our student-driven organization, we focus on nurturing creativity and technical skills through photography and coding competitions. We provide a platform where students can showcase their talents, learn from each other, and receive constructive feedback. Our aim is to empower participants to explore their passions, develop their abilities, and foster a supportive community of learning and growth in photography and coding.

Photography

We aim to empower aspiring photographers by providing them with the knowledge and tools to hone their craft and capture stunning images that tell compelling stories.

Events

Our events center around engaging photography and coding competitions, offering participants opportunities to showcase their creative eye behind the lens and their technical abilities in coding. These competitions foster a supportive environment for students to learn, grow, and excel in these dynamic fields

Coding

We're focused on empowering students to enhance their coding skills through accessible resources and personalized support, fostering a journey of growth and creativity in programming.
